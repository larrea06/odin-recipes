Lasagna

Description

Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about an hour.

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for eight to ten minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
